🔍 TROUBLESHOOTING:

❌ Port 3000 in use?
   pkill -f "bun.*backend/server.ts"

❌ Database errors?
   rm vibesync.db
   ./START_ALL.sh

❌ Module not found?
   bun install
   ./START_ALL.sh
