Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Go to Settings → Security → Change Password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be deleted within 30 days.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Tap the three-dot menu on any post, comment, or profile and select "Report". You can also email abuse@vibesync.app with details.
How do I block or unblock someone?
Go to their profile → three-dot menu → Block. To unblock, go to Settings → Privacy → Blocked Accounts.
Why can't I see someone's posts?
They may have blocked you, made their account private, or deleted their posts. You can also check if you've muted them in Settings → Privacy → Muted Accounts.
How do I download my data?
Go to Settings → Privacy → Download Your Data. We'll email you a copy of your data within 48 hours.
Is VibeSync free?
Yes! VibeSync is completely free to use with no hidden charges.
What devices are supported?
VibeSync works on iOS 13+, Android 8+, and modern web browsers.
How do I enable/disable notifications?
Go to Settings → Notifications and customize which notifications you want to receive.
Can I use VibeSync on multiple devices?
Yes! Log in with the same account on any device. You can manage active sessions in Settings → Security → Active Sessions.
